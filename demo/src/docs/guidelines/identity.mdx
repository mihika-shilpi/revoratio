## The Name

To maintain consistency across countries and languages, the name of the school is always written the same way: Title Case, with an accents on the first É. The school name is followed by the country or city of the school in question.

1. Do not drop the Ecole.
2. Do not abbreviate.
3. Do not refer to solely as Ecole.
4. Do not drop the lab
5. Do not use lowercase at the start of a word
6. Do not use a period between words.

## The Logos

The various editions of the logos are to be used based on scenario. The simplest is the solo logo, which is used in most situations.

### Solo

The following guidelines for the 3 versions of every logo unit apply to all logo units.

![Identity%20f12c624b74734dcdada7c224060995e0/Group_33942x.png](Identity%20f12c624b74734dcdada7c224060995e0/Group_33942x.png)

**Transparent**
This is to be used on undisturbed, light backgrounds at large scale only.

![Identity%20f12c624b74734dcdada7c224060995e0/Group_33952x.png](Identity%20f12c624b74734dcdada7c224060995e0/Group_33952x.png)

**Solid**
Used most frequently, on any range of backgrounds and at any size.

![Identity%20f12c624b74734dcdada7c224060995e0/Group_33962x.png](Identity%20f12c624b74734dcdada7c224060995e0/Group_33962x.png)

**Inverted**
For exclusive use as a watermark on photos or videos, after the logo has already been presented.

### With Tagline

On document headers, page headers, or other initial screens, a logo with tagline exists for 3 countries. This is to be used only 1 per design at maximum. The logos are available in several different lock ups, to be used based on support orientation and proportion.
